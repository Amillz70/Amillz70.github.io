Hello There

I am Adam

I am a web developer who graduated, from Union College, with a degree in Economics. I spent the past four years working in accounting and finance. I enjoy the problem solving aspects of the finance world, but not the means of getting there. I knew I needed a change in career, but not what that change needed to be. After taking a Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly, I look forward to the new challenges that this career shift brings with it.

Cups of Coffee (and Counting)

I started my career transition when I enrolled in a coding bootcamp at General Assembly. It was a 12 week full time immersive program. We learned a number of languages and frameworks, we were also tasked to create a portfolio containing 4 projects. The first project was a Tic-Tac-Toe game. For the second project I created a fantasy football managing application. The third project was a group project. My team was given the task of creating a Survey Manager site. For my capstone project, I decided to create a messaging application that would respond in real time.

General Assembly became a place for me to really delve into code and grow as a programmer. It was beneficial learning in a collaborative environment, where everyone was looking for new career paths. GA projects allow students to show what we learned as well as to teach us that we are capable of coding incredible things.
